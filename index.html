Welcome to gingeralma

We are a traditional powdered drink manufacturer dedicated to reviving the tradition of drinking ginger, turmeric, kunyit asem, temulawak, beras kencur and red ginger in a practical and enjoyable way. We are proud to serve high quality instant powdered drinks that can be easily enjoyed by anyone, anywhere.

Vision

Our vision is to reintroduce the health benefits of natural ingredients such as ginger, turmeric, 'curcuma', 'kencur' and red ginger to the busy modern society. We believe that powdered traditional drinks are the ideal way passed down from generation to generation by Indonesian ancestors to maintain health and well-being without having to sacrifice your time.

Our products are made with care using carefully selected high quality spices

Organic

Discover high quality instant drinks from gingeralma, without the addition of any chemicals. Made 100% pure from traditional spices extracted from Indonesia's natural wealth

Convenient and easy to use

Gingeralma provides traditional drinks in ready-to-drink instant powder form, making it easier for you to prepare and enjoy them anytime, anywhere. Just mix it with hot or cold water. You can even add other ingredients like honey, lemon etc. according to your taste

Authentic Indonesian Flavors, “Enak Berkhasiat”.

Gingeralma offers a range of traditional instant powdered drinks including red ginger, turmeric, kunyit asem, temulawak, beras kencur' 'jakute, which allows you to enjoy the delicious authentic taste of Indonesia and at the same time get excellent benefits for your body's health, which is already famous for a long time.
